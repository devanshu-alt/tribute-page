Life & Achievements

  • Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • Played a leading role in developing India's Agni and Prithvi missiles.
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor, in 1997.
  • Authored many inspirational books including Wings of Fire.
